Chapter 2

James struggled to learn Paldean language, but he eventually managed to adapt and also made a few good friends. However, he could not tolerate food on Paldea. He became sick and had to miss a few weeks of academy. To his surprise, the main diet of Paldean was soil and stones.

Choose his next action.

He found a ramen restaurant, which did not taste as good as one on his home planet, but he kept going there.

He forced himself to continue eating soil and stones.

He found a supermarket which sells human food and started cooking on his own.
