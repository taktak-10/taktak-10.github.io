New Life

James struggled to learn Paldean language and follow the lessons and lectures, but he eventually managed them and also made a few good friends. However, he could not tolerate the food in Paldea. He soon became sick and had to miss a few weeks of academy. To his surprise, the main diet of Paldeans was soil and stones. After he recovered from the sickness,

he found a ramen restaurant, which did not taste as good as one in his home planet, but he got addicted to it.

he forced himself to continue eating soil and stones.

he found a supermarket which sells human food and started cooking on his own.
